Aspiring to be a customized software fork of the US government's official <a href="https://pages.18f.gov/identity-intro/">Login.gov platform</a>. It will be a high-assurance identity system for Canadians, achieved through postal mail verification of voting addresses. Third-party platforms will be able to use it for sign-in, though unlike Facebook, it can corroborate membership in geographic districts.
